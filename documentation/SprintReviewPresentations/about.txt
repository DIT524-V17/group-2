Sprint Review Presentations


This section contains brief presentations of what has been done throughout the individual Sprints


The presentations' format:	
*Title card 		- contains Group name/number, participating team memebers
*Plan			- contains the initial plan for this sprint in the form of goals/objectives to be achieved
*Acomplishments		- a list of goals/objectives that were managed to be achieved
*Next Sprint		- describes what will be worked on the following sprint
*Areas of improvment	- states some of the problems or areas that could be improved in the future. Code/communication/work proccess are taken into account
